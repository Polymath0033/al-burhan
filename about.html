About Us

Home About Us

Al-Burhan Media, is a fast-rising islamic Media organisation aimed at providing the Muslim Ummah with Optimum Islamic Media contents. In the world today, we have not less than 10,000 satellite television stations on earth with about one-third of them being religion satellite TV stations. They need to pass pristine undiluted knowledge of islam from the correct sources across the globe lead to the birth of Al-Burhan Media in 2018

Owing a wide range of media outreah, we make efforts to ensure the dissemination of pristine islam, quality information, and vivacious knowledge in various aspects of the Deen. Using our different media platforms, we deliver daily videos of the Qur'an regular episodes of videos from Sunni scholars; translated in and transcribed in English, posting articles and translated books of scholars of islam on our website, hosting of podcasts, attending the welfare of of Muslims around the world and breaking the bridge between scholars and their students by providing means of asking question and scholars provide answer to them.
we hope our media would be first to have an HD/7 satellite TV channel and radio station in West Africa, which should used in pleading pristine islam to the grassroots and untouched/unaware part of the continent and around the globe at large.
It was initially established in 2018, but was registered and operational 2022

Mission and Vision

Vision

Our Vision: "Correcting the wrong practice of our Ummah"
"Connecting the modern world to the divinely revealed Religion"
At Al-Burhan Media, our hope is for the followers to practice islam in accordance with the book of Allah, The Glorious Qur'an, the authentic practices and traditions of the Prophet Muhammad , and his companions, may Allah be pleased with them all. This demonstrate our wish for Muslims all over the world to be well-versed in the knowledge of basic and in-depth principles of Islam, as well as being informed of past, and present occurrences of the Muslim Ummah

Mission

"Sending pristine message of the islam to Entirety of Human Race"
Al-Burhan Media aims to create better awareness and understanding of islam to preserve the teaching our noble prophet(S.A.W) and ensure an understanding of the Deen by the Ummah. Our call is to disseminate pristine pristine islam to the enirety of man. We work to remove ignorance and doubts about islam and bring people out of the darkness of falsehood into the light of islam with the permission of Allah
